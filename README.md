**StackInsights** is an internal knowledge-sharing platform designed to help teams document, discover, and learn from technical insights, incident reports, retrospectives, and best practices.
It provides a centralized, searchable, blog-style experience where contributors can publish learnings and teams can easily access past knowledge across the organization.

The platform focuses on improving knowledge visibility, reducing repeated mistakes, and fostering a culture of shared learning.

## ğŸ¯ Problem Statement

In fast-growing engineering teams, valuable learnings from incidents, optimizations, and retrospectives are often scattered across documents, chats, or ticketing tools. This makes it difficult to:

* Discover past insights
* Learn from previous incidents
* Reuse proven solutions

**StackInsights** solves this by offering a structured, searchable, and team-centric knowledge hub.
